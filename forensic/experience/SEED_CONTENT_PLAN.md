## SEED CONTENT BATCH 1: ARTICLES (Content Library)

**Format for each:** Title, Category, Summary (100–150 words), Key Scripture

---

### Article 1: "The Holy Spirit Is Not a Feeling — He Is a Person"

**Category:** Teaching / Holy Spirit
**Summary:**
Many believers approach the Holy Spirit as an experience to chase — a warmth, a sensation, a goosebump. But Acts 1:8 doesn't say "you will feel power." It says you WILL RECEIVE power. The Holy Spirit is the third Person of the Trinity — not a theological concept to debate but a Presence to encounter and obey. This teaching explores what it means to cultivate an ongoing relationship with the Spirit, yield to His voice in daily decisions, and carry His fire beyond the church walls into your home, workplace, and generation.

**Key Scripture:** Acts 1:8 — "But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you."
**Estimated read time:** 7 minutes
**Author:** W. Washika

---

### Article 2: "Why Africa Is the Next Great Revival Epicenter"

**Category:** Vision / Movement
**Summary:**
Church history records revival as a pattern — not an accident. It has followed prayer, hunger, and a people willing to be used. In this piece, we examine the spiritual trajectory of Africa: a continent where Christianity is growing faster than anywhere else on earth, where prayer mountains never close, where the young are not post-Christian but pre-revival. Africa is not waiting for the West's next move. The fire is already being lit from within. Here is why Spirit Revival Africa believes this is the continent's defining hour — and what every believer can do to carry the flame.

**Key Scripture:** Isaiah 60:1 — "Arise, shine, for your light has come."
**Estimated read time:** 6 minutes
**Author:** W. Washika

---

### Article 3: "How to Build a Prayer Life That Doesn't Collapse"

**Category:** Prayer / Practical
**Summary:**
Most believers start strong in prayer and then drift. Not because they stopped believing — but because they never built a structure. This teaching gives a practical, no-guilt framework for building a consistent prayer life grounded in scripture and sustainable through seasons of dryness. Covering the "Morning Watch" concept, the power of intercession for others before praying for yourself, and how to pray with the Word so your prayers stop feeling like monologues and start feeling like conversations with a God who answers. Real faith. Practical tools. No performance required.

**Key Scripture:** Matthew 6:6 — "But when you pray, go into your room, close the door..."
**Estimated read time:** 8 minutes
**Author:** W. Washika

---

### Article 4: "What Revival Actually Looks Like in an African City"

**Category:** Stories / Vision
**Summary:**
Revival is not always a tent meeting and falling bodies. Sometimes it looks like fifteen young professionals gathering on a Wednesday night in Nairobi to pray for their offices. Sometimes it's a WhatsApp group that starts praying at midnight and doesn't stop until 3am. Sometimes it's a university student who becomes the first believer in his family and changes the spiritual temperature of an entire home. This article explores what real, on-the-ground revival looks like in the streets, estates, and workplaces of African cities — and why it starts with you.

**Key Scripture:** Acts 2:17 — "In the last days, God says, I will pour out my Spirit on all people."
**Estimated read time:** 5 minutes
**Author:** SRA Team

---

### Article 5: "The Calling Doesn't Wait for Credentials"

**Category:** Encouragement / Identity
**Summary:**
W. Washika was 25 years old and working as a land surveyor when God gave him a mandate to reignite revival across a continent. He wasn't a seminary graduate. He didn't have a church building or a budget. He had an encounter and a yes. This piece is for every young African believer who has felt the stirring of something bigger than their circumstances — a calling too large for their resources, a vision that doesn't fit their current season. The anointing doesn't wait. It calls you into readiness so it can catch you off guard with what it was always going to build through you.

**Key Scripture:** Jeremiah 1:7 — "Do not say, 'I am too young.'"
**Estimated read time:** 6 minutes
**Author:** W. Washika

## SEED CONTENT BATCH 4: DISCIPLESHIP COURSE (1 Full Outline)

**Course Title:** "Foundations of Fire — A 3-Week Course for New Believers"

**Description:**
For every person who said yes to God and then wasn't sure what to do next. Three weeks. Three lessons. A lifetime of foundation.

**Lesson 1: Who Is the Holy Spirit?**
- Objective: Move from "concept" to "Person"
- Content: Acts 1–2, John 14:16–17, 26
- Exercise: Write a one-paragraph letter to the Holy Spirit as if meeting Him for the first time
- Duration: 20 minutes

**Lesson 2: How to Pray Like the Early Church**
- Objective: Build a structured, sustainable daily prayer practice
- Content: Acts 2:42–47, Luke 18:1–8, Matthew 6:5–13
- Exercise: Complete the 7-day "Morning Watch" prayer challenge
- Duration: 25 minutes

**Lesson 3: Your Identity in Christ — The Crown You Already Wear**
- Objective: Establish identity-first theology before mission
- Content: Drawn from "Beneath the Crown" Ch. 1–3, Romans 8:14–17
- Exercise: Write a personal "Revival Statement" — 3 sentences about who you are in Christ
- Duration: 30 minutes

**Assessment:** None (remove barriers). Completion = self-reported.
**Certificate:** Optional digital badge "Foundations of Fire — Week 1 Complete"

---

**Production guidance:**
- Phone camera is FINE. Good lighting > expensive camera.
- A ring light facing the founder + natural background = professional enough.
- Captions are mandatory (80% of mobile video is watched muted).
- Upload to YouTube (unlisted or public) and embed on homepage via modal.
